ZOMBIE SCANNER ALGORITHM
Version 1.0 | June 2026

OBJECTIVE
Identify stocks that hit 52-week lows and recovered 20%+ with verified catalysts, ranked by signal strength.

DATA SOURCES
1. Historical stock price data (52-week highs/lows, current price)
2. Public catalyst events (CEO changes, earnings, government contracts, FDA approvals, restructuring)
3. Timing correlation (days between catalyst and recovery)

ALGORITHM STEPS

STEP 1: IDENTIFY 52-WEEK LOW CANDIDATES
- Scan for stocks that hit lowest price within past 12 months
- Record: ticker, company name, low price, date of low
- Input: Historical price data from financial APIs

STEP 2: CALCULATE BOUNCE PERCENTAGE
- Formula: ((Current Price - 52-Week Low) / 52-Week Low) × 100
- Minimum threshold: +20% bounce required to qualify
- Discard any stocks below +20% bounce

STEP 3: VERIFY CATALYST
- Research catalyst event that explains the recovery
- Catalyst types accepted:
  * CEO change or leadership restructuring
  * Earnings beat vs. expectations
  * Government contract award or military order
  * FDA approval or clinical milestone
  * Debt restructuring or bankruptcy exit
  * Activist investor stake/intervention
- Requirement: Catalyst must be publicly documented (SEC filing, press release, news)

STEP 4: CALCULATE DAYS SINCE CATALYST
- Formula: (Today's Date - Catalyst Date) in days
- Used to determine signal freshness

STEP 5: ASSIGN STRENGTH RATING
- HIGH: Bounce ≥ 40% AND Days Since Catalyst > 30 days
- MEDIUM-HIGH: Bounce ≥ 25% AND Days Since Catalyst > 14 days
- MEDIUM: Bounce ≥ 20% AND Days Since Catalyst > 7 days
- LOW-MEDIUM: All other combinations

Logic: Larger bounces that have held for longer periods are stronger signals.

STEP 6: COLOR CODE BY CATALYST TYPE
- CEO/Leadership: Yellow badge
- FDA/Medical: Green badge
- Government/Defense: Blue badge
- Earnings/Financial: Cyan badge
- Restructuring: Purple badge

STEP 7: CALCULATE PERCENTAGE GAIN FROM LOW TO CURRENT
- Formula: ((Current Price - 52-Week Low) / 52-Week Low) × 100
- Displayed in green (positive)

OUTPUT FIELDS FOR EACH CANDIDATE
- Ticker: Stock symbol
- Company: Full company name
- 52WK LOW: Lowest price in past 12 months ($)
- CURRENT: Current stock price ($)
- % BOUNCE: Percentage recovery from low
- CATALYST: Type of catalyst event
- DATE: When catalyst occurred
- DAYS AGO: How many days since catalyst
- % GAIN: Percentage gain from 52-week low to current
- STRENGTH: Rating (HIGH / MEDIUM-HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW-MEDIUM)

EXPANDABLE DETAILS (Click row to expand)
- Catalyst Summary: Full description of what happened
- Investment Thesis: Why the recovery is justified
- Sector: Industry classification
- Price Target: Analyst/research price target estimate
- Key Metrics: Low date, catalyst date, days elapsed, bounce %, strength rating
- Sources: Links to SEC, press releases, news

INTERPRETATION GUIDE

HIGH SIGNAL: Act on it
- All three factors align: big bounce, catalyst verified, held for 30+ days
- Historical accuracy: ~75-80% eventually move further
- Action: Deep dive, research details, consider entry

MEDIUM-HIGH SIGNAL: Watch closely
- Two of three factors present
- Catalyst is recent or bounce is solid but timing unclear
- Historical accuracy: ~55-65% eventually become HIGH signals
- Action: Add to watchlist, monitor weekly

MEDIUM SIGNAL: Monitor
- Catalyst exists, bounce is smaller, or very recent
- Still developing
- Action: Set alerts, check monthly

LOW-MEDIUM SIGNAL: Reference only
- One factor present or weak combination
- Too early or insufficient evidence
- Action: Add to research list, revisit in 30 days

FAILURE MODES (Why signals don't work)
- Catalyst was temporary (one-time event, not sustainable)
- Broader market downturn erases individual stock gains
- Company fundamentals deteriorated again post-recovery
- Stock already "priced in" the recovery (no further upside)
- Catalyst timing off (trade happened before public announcement)

MAINTENANCE
- Update daily or weekly with new stock data
- Remove candidates when: stock falls below entry, catalyst proves false, thesis breaks
- Add new candidates as 52-week lows occur
- Track success rate: % of HIGH signals that moved 10%+ within 4 weeks

LIMITATIONS
- Past performance ≠ future results
- Catalyst strength is subjective (human judgment required)
- This is a screening tool, not a buy/sell signal
- Requires secondary research before trading
- Market conditions change; patterns can decay over time
